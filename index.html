What’s BlocksLab?

BlocksLab is like a mad scientist’s playground where we mess around with 5 to 100 creatures every single day. We check ‘em out, pick a champ, and BOOM – drop 100,000 $BLOCKS in their pocket. It’s a 24-hour cycle of discovery and rewards.

How It Works

Creature Drop-Off: Each day, creatures roll into our lab – could be AI stuff, art, projects, whatever.

Research Mode On: Our team of brainiacs runs the creatures through tests. Think vibes, performance, and uniqueness.

Crown the Champ: By the end of the day, we choose the MVP. The winner takes home 100,000 $BLOCKS.

Repeat the Madness: Reset, refresh, and do it all over again the next day.
